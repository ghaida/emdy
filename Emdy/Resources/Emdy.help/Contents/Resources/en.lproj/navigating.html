Reading and navigating

Heading navigator

Press ⇧⌘H or choose View > Toggle Headings to open the heading navigator sidebar. It lists all headings in the document. Click a heading to jump to that section.

Minimap

Press ⇧⌘M or choose View > Toggle Minimap to show a scaled overview of the document along the right edge. Click or drag within the minimap to scroll to that position.

Find in page

Press ⌘F to open the find bar. Type to search incrementally through the document.

  • ⌘G — Find next match
  • ⇧⌘G — Find previous match

Anchor links

If a Markdown document contains internal links to headings (anchor links), clicking one scrolls to the corresponding heading.
